Frequently Asked Questions


What is MYTEL ?

Mytel is a major telecommunications company in Myanmar (Burma), as one of four national carriers.

It provides a lot of facilities to users across the country.You can easily do all the actions for recharge like recharge your mobiles,pay bills,create your own Mytel id,convert your sim from prepaid to post and postpaid to prepaid.

Who Developed this MYTEL ?

Btech Students(Bibhudatta Behera,Somit Das,Ranjit Rout,Lipsita Priyadarshini Behera) from Templecity Institute of Technology & Engineering.

How do I check my remaining balances or to subscribe again ? Do I have to memorize a lot of ways to do so ?

No! Absolutely not! All you need to remember is our short code: *966#. All the functions you need such as checking remaining balance, subscribing to the package again, adding or removing auto-renew features from packages and unsubscribing can be done using this syntax.

What will happen to my Data if I have not finished using them before the expiration date ?

The remaining unused pack balance will be rolled-over to the next purchase if you buy within 7 days after the pack has expired.

Can I buy both Social Pack and Night Internet Pack Simultaneously if I Have Jio Sim ?

Yes, you can. When you use the Internet between 11PM and 7AM, the deduction will first happen from the active Night Internet Pack.

My telephone can’t access the memory card or access but error. What can I do ?

In which countries can I use data roaming if I subscribe Data roaming promotion package in India ?

You can only use this data roaming promotion in India.
